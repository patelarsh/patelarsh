My Story

Success Story

View Company Page

In the quiet village of Kesharpura, young Patel Arsh was born on December 8, 2006. His family, residing in Kesharpura, consisted of his father Mahammadsalim, a diligent Clark/VCE at Kesharpura Gram Panchayat, and his mother YasminBen, who served as an English teacher. Arsh, being the beloved son of the household, grew up with dreams and aspirations shaped by his grandfather, who envisioned him becoming a doctor.

However, Arsh's interests took a different turn. Despite his grandfather's dream, Arsh found himself drawn to the world of computers and nurtured a dream of becoming a software engineer. His academic journey began at the Village Primary School, where he quickly distinguished himself as a clever student.

As fate would have it, the COVID-19 pandemic struck during Arsh's 8th class, leading to a prolonged lockdown and the closure of schools and colleges. Seizing the opportunity, Arsh delved into online coding courses, fueled by his newfound passion. Although the 8th-class exams were cleared through mass promotion, Arsh transferred to Ashish Vidhyalay School for his secondary education.

Despite the challenges posed by the ongoing pandemic, Arsh remained steadfast in his pursuit of knowledge. He continued learning to code during the extended lockdown and managed to successfully transition to the 9th class when schools eventually reopened. The resilience and determination displayed during this time bore fruit when Arsh achieved mass promotion once again due to the prevailing circumstances.

Moving on to the 10th class, Arsh faced a dilemma. Uninterested in subjects unrelated to computer studies, he decided to specialize in Diploma in Information Technology after 10th. His father, Mahammadsalim, supported his decision, urging him to focus on scoring 80+ in the 10th board exams.

Following the board exams, Arsh embarked on a learning journey, relocating to the nearby city of Himmatnagar to delve deeper into coding. His skills flourished, and by 2022, he founded SpaceOn Technology, a software company providing top-notch IT solutions. Arsh simultaneously pursued a diploma in Information Technology at L.J Polytechnic, where he gained fame as a FullStack Developer in a short span.

However, Arsh's thirst for knowledge didn't end there. A year later, he ventured into the realm of Ethical Hacking and Cyber Security. By the third semester of his college, Arsh had become a proficient Ethical Hacker, solving complex security issues within his college's LMS panel.

SpaceOn Technology thrived under Arsh's leadership, completing over 100 projects from 2020 to 2023 and continuing to flourish into 2024. Patel Arsh's remarkable journey from a small village to a successful entrepreneur and ethical hacker exemplifies the power of passion, determination, and adaptability in the face of challenges.
